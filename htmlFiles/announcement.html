Circular 061: Disaster fund

In response to the massive landslides that struck Wayanad, on July 30, 2024, our college is mobilizing funds to support those affected by this tragic disaster.
The magnitude of this disaster has caused an immediate need for essential supplies and support.
Interested faculties / students can contribute to the relief fund.

The class tutors are asked to collect money from the students who are willing to contribute and maintain a name list.
We urge everyone to contribute generously and assist in this crucial effort to save lives and aid recovery.
And, also asked to submit the collected money to the college fees counter.

Last date for submission: August 07, 2024.
